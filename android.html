Welcome to PCollect Android Early Access!

Thank you for trying out PCollect during its early access phase. We truly appreciate your support and feedback as we continue to improve the app.

To get started, please follow these steps:

  1. Join the Google group by clicking here.
  2. Download the app from the Google Play Store by clicking here.
  3. Even if you encounter issues or have suggestions for improvement, we kindly request that you keep the app installed on your device for at least two weeks. This will help us gather valuable data and insights to refine the app before its public release. We are committed to enhancing PCollect based on your feedback, which you can provide by filling out our feedback form.

Once again, thank you for being a part of our early access community. Your participation is crucial in shaping the future of PCollect!
